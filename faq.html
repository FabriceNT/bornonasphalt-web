Frequently asked

What material are the shirts?

Comfort Colors 1717 — a garment-dyed heavyweight tee. It's soft, durable, and holds up to washing far better than a standard thin tee.

Where do you ship?

US domestic only, for now. Shipping is a flat $4.75 per order, and free on orders of $85 or more — the cart shows you the exact total before checkout.

How long does an order take to arrive?

Each shirt is printed on demand through Printful. Production typically takes 2–5 business days, then 3–5 business days in transit within the US.

Can I return or exchange a design?

Yes — see our Shipping & Returns page for the full policy on print-on-demand returns and exchanges.

Do you make designs for other muscle car models?

New designs drop in phases across Ford, Mopar, and GM. Sign up with your email at the welcome popup to get notified before each drop.

Do I need an account to order?

No — guest checkout is available. Creating an account just lets you save your addresses and see order history.
