Shipping & returns

Shipping

We currently ship US domestic only. Shipping is a flat $4.75 per order — and free on orders of $85 or more, calculated automatically in your cart before checkout.

Each item is printed on demand through Printful. Production typically takes 2–5 business days, followed by 3–5 business days in transit. You'll receive a tracking number by email once your order ships.

Returns & exchanges

Because every shirt is printed to order, we're not able to accept returns for reasons of taste or fit once printing has started. If your item arrives damaged, misprinted, or the wrong size due to our error, contact us within 14 days of delivery and we'll replace it at no cost.

To start a claim, use the Contact page with your order number and a photo of the issue.

Order tracking

Once your order ships, you'll get a confirmation email with a tracking link. If you created an account, tracking is also available in your order history.
